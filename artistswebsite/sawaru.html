sawaru

Sawaru consists of two independent cylinders touching in a perpendicular direction. One plays the role as a light source, while the other acts as a base, and the projection angle can be altered into 3 stages: 25 degrees, 40 degrees and 60 degrees, by inserting the pin attached to the base. Furthermore, by removing the base, the light source can be laid onto the floor for direct illumination.
