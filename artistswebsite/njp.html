njp table

The “NJP table’s” unique shade lights up one’s desktop directly, while also letting light out through a chimney-like hole at the top, glowing up the arm with a warm and soothing indirect light. The light shade is made out of heat-radiating aluminium, while the upper hole releases heat effectively. You can therefore touch the shade to change the direction or position of the lamp with ease and precision. The on/off switch has also been positioned at the point where your fingers touch when clasping the shade.
