proteca 360

A polycarbonate suitcase with a zip fastener running all the way around, letting you open it from any direction. A raised frame inside increases the strength of the shell, keeps rain from seeping in through the zip fastener, and allows for ample storage space without a centre-split construction. The design also provides easy access to separators and pockets even when the suitcase is in an upright position. This flagship model is the first release and ultimate embodiment of the proteca reborn brand.
