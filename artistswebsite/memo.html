block memo

Three different shape sticky notes, which have been unified into a singular shape comprised of a square, a large rectangle and a small rectangle. It consists of a form of a cube with one of the corners cut off, and these sticky notes can be peeled off as it stands in a slanted position. This has facilitated the use of these notes in assorted sizes for people to use for various purposes, such as using them as memos, as bookmarks and so forth.
