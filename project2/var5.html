I Made Myself a Snowball
Shel Silverstein, Falling Up

                 I made

                     myself a
     snowball,
            as perfect as could be. I
                         thought I’d keep it as a pet,
 and let it

sleep with me. I made


  it some pajamas And a

pillow
                   for its head. Then last night
       it ran away, But           first
    it
                 
wet
the
bed
!
