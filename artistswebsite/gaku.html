A frame with lamps and other objects. An adjustable-height pending lamp hangs down inside of one frame, while a second design features non-contact charging functionality to power a matching spot or luminescent lamp. The directional lamp features a magnet that allows it to be placed at an angle. Settings include a mode where lights are turned only when sitting on the charging dock so that the location of the lighting serves as a power switch. The accessories are held in place by magnets.
